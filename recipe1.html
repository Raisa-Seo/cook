Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 4 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside, reserving 1/2 cup of pasta water.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add garlic and saut√© until golden brown, about 2 minutes.
  3. Add red pepper flakes and a pinch of salt, then toss in the cooked spaghetti.
  4. Mix well, adding pasta water a little at a time if necessary to make a silky sauce.
  5. Garnish with fresh parsley (optional) and serve hot.
